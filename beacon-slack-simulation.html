Interpersonal Savvy · The how
Generating Energy Without Authority: The Exothermic Leadership Model
A Beacon-generated micro-lesson on how senior individual contributors build cross-functional trust and influence without positional power — grounded in Stripe's exothermic operating principle.
Micro-lesson
At Stripe, Interpersonal Savvy isn't about being likeable — it's about being generative. The most influential senior ICs don't just do great work; they leave every interaction having made the people around them clearer, more capable, or more confident.

This is what Stripe calls exothermic: energy that releases outward, not inward. For a senior engineer, it looks like: naming what you've learned in a retro so others benefit, flagging your approach before you start rather than after you finish, or asking a PM what they need from you at the top of a planning session instead of waiting to be asked.

None of these require authority. All of them build it.
Reflection prompt
In your next 1:1 with this direct report, ask: "When do you feel most connected to the work the broader team is doing? What gets in the way of that?"
